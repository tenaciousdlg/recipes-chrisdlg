steps:
  - "Poach the chicken: bring the water (or broth) to a boil in a large pot. Add the chicken breast, half the cilantro sprigs, half the onion, 1 garlic clove, and half the salt. Simmer ~15 min until the chicken is cooked through (165F)."
  - Remove the chicken, cilantro, onion, and garlic from the broth (reserve the broth). Shred the chicken.
  - "Blend the tomato sauce: blend the tomatoes, the remaining garlic clove, the remaining onion, and 1 cup of the reserved broth until smooth."
  - "Cook the tomato sauce: heat a third of the olive oil in a pot over medium. Add the blended tomato sauce and cook ~5 min, stirring occasionally."
  - In a separate pot, heat the remaining olive oil. Add the fideo and saute until light golden brown.
  - Add the reserved broth to the fideo pot, then the tomato sauce, the chicken bouillon, and the remaining cilantro sprigs. Boil ~8 min until the fideo is cooked.
  - Remove the cilantro sprigs. Stir in the shredded chicken and salt to taste.
  - Serve hot, garnished with queso fresco (or parmesan) and a squeeze of lime.
