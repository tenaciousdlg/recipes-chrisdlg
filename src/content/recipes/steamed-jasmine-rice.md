steps:
  - Rinse the rice in a fine-mesh strainer under cold water, swishing with your fingers, until the water runs mostly clear. This washes off the surface starch that makes rice gummy.
  - Combine the rinsed rice and water in a pot. 1 cup to 1.25 cups water is the usual ratio; use closer to 1 cup water for firmer rice, 1.25 for softer.
  - Bring to a boil uncovered, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer 15-18 min without lifting the lid.
  - Remove from heat and let rest, covered, 5-10 min to finish steaming.
  - Fluff with a fork before serving.

## Origin

The everyday method: rinse until the water runs clear, then simmer at roughly 1 cup rice to a
cup to a cup and a quarter of water. No rice cooker needed.
