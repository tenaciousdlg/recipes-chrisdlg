steps:
  - Brown the andouille in 1 tbsp oil in a Dutch oven ~8 min. Remove with a slotted spoon, drain.
  - Season the chicken with Creole seasoning; brown in batches in the fat, 5-6 min. Remove, cool, refrigerate until needed.
  - "Make the roux: combine the 1/2 cup oil and flour in the same pot over medium. Stir slowly and constantly 20-25 min until it's the color of chocolate. This is the soul of gumbo; don't rush or scorch it."
  - Add onions, celery, bell pepper; cook until wilted, 4-5 min. Add the sausage, salt, cayenne, bay leaves; cook 2 min.
  - Slowly add the stock, stirring; bring to a boil, reduce to medium-low, cook uncovered 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  - Add the chicken and simmer 1.5 hours, skimming fat. Remove chicken, shred off the bone, return the meat.
  - Off heat, stir in scallions, parsley, and file powder. Serve over white rice with hot sauce.
