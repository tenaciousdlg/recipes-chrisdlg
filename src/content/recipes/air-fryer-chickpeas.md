steps:
  - Rinse and drain the chickpeas well. For extra crisp, pat dry between paper towels (optional, the air fryer does most of the drying).
  - Toss the chickpeas with 2 tsp of the oil to coat.
  - Preheat the air fryer to 400F (or run it empty 2-3 min).
  - "Air fry in a single layer 5 min, shake, another 5 min, shake, then check: add 2-3 more min to your preferred crispness (~13 min total is the sweet spot, when they crack open like popcorn)."
  - Transfer to a bowl, drizzle the last 1 tsp oil to help seasoning stick, add your seasoning and salt, toss. Eat warm and crispy.
