steps:
  - Dice the onion and mince the garlic. Saute both in the olive oil over medium heat until the onion is soft and translucent, ~5 min.
  - Add the tomato paste and cook 3-5 min more, stirring constantly, until it darkens slightly.
  - Add the crushed tomatoes, basil, oregano, brown sugar, and salt. Stir to combine.
  - Bring to a gentle simmer, then reduce to medium-low. Partially cover and simmer ~30 min, stirring occasionally.
  - Taste and adjust seasoning. If too acidic, simmer longer (up to 1 hour) or add a bit more sugar. Serve hot, or refrigerate/freeze.
