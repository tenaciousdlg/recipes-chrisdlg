steps:
  - Preheat oven to 400F. Peel and cube the sweet potato. Put it and the frozen broccoli (no thaw) on a large sheet pan.
  - Drizzle with olive oil; season the sweet potato with seasoned salt and the broccoli with salt and pepper. Toss to coat, keeping them on separate sides. Roast 15 min.
  - Meanwhile mix the meatloaf, ground beef, egg, bread crumbs, 1 tbsp BBQ sauce, smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt. Divide in two and shape flattened ovals.
  - After 15 min, stir the veg and push aside. Add the meatloaves in the center, spread 1 tbsp BBQ sauce over each.
  - Roast another 20 min until the meatloaves hit 160F. Serve.
