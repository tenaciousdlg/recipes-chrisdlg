steps:
  - Saute onion, jalapeno, and garlic in olive oil in a large pot until soft, ~5 min. Stir in cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, and salt; bloom 1 min.
  - Add fire-roasted tomatoes and chicken broth. Nestle in the whole chicken thighs. Bring to a simmer and cook ~20 min until the chicken is tender.
  - Remove the chicken, shred with two forks, return to the pot. Add the black beans and simmer another 5-10 min.
  - Off heat, add the lime juice. Taste and adjust salt/heat.
  - Serve topped with avocado, crispy tortilla strips, and cilantro. Cheese or Greek yogurt optional.
