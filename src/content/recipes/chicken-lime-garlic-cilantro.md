steps:
  - Preheat oven to 375F. Pat thighs dry, season both sides with cumin, salt, and pepper.
  - Heat oil in a heavy skillet or Dutch oven over medium-high. Sear thighs skin-side down until deeply browned, 5-7 min; don't crowd, work in batches. Turn, brown 3-5 min more. Remove and set aside.
  - Lower heat, leave ~1.5 tbsp fat. Add flour, garlic, and scallion whites; stir to coat and cook 2-3 min. Whisk in the wine and simmer 3 min, scraping up the brown bits.
  - Add broth, honey, lime juice, lime zest, and half the cilantro. Bring to a simmer.
  - Return the thighs skin-side up, uncovered, into the oven. Roast 35-40 min until 165F and the skin is set.
  - Garnish with sliced lime, remaining cilantro, and scallion greens. Serve with rice, potatoes, or crusty bread for the sauce.
