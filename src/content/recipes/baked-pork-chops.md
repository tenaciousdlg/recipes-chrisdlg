steps:

  - Place the pork chops on a cutting board and rub with the olive oil.
  - Stir together the salt, black pepper, smoked paprika, and onion powder in a small bowl. Rub all sides of the pork chops with the seasoning mix. Place on the prepared baking sheet.
  - Bake for 15 to 20 minutes, or until the pork chops reach an internal temperature of 145F (actual time depends on thickness). Serve hot.

## Origin

A simple sheet-pan pork chop recipe built around a four-spice rub, no marinating or brining
required. Straightforward weeknight protein that also makes an easy one-pan meal with roasted
vegetables added to the same sheet.
