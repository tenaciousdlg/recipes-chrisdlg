steps:
  - Peel and slice the carrots into 1/4-inch pieces, on a diagonal for a nicer shape. Slice the red onion and jalapeno the same thickness.
  - Add the water, vinegar, oil, cumin, oregano, salt, and pepper to a pot. Bring the brine to a boil.
  - "Carefully add the sliced vegetables to the boiling brine, then boil ~5 min: don't let the carrots get too soft or the color fade too much."
  - Remove from heat and transfer the vegetables and all the brine into jars or a heat-proof container.
  - Eat right away if you can't wait, but the flavor is better after a day in the fridge. Keeps 3-4 weeks refrigerated.
