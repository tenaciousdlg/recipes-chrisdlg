## Origin

A family sauce recipe, scaled to cover 2 lbs of protein. Sweet-savory-bright (orange + honey +
soy + vinegar) with a whisper of chili. A natural platform: swap chicken for salmon, shrimp, or
tofu with no change to the sauce.
