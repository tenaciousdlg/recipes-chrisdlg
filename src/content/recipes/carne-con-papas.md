## Origin

Formalized from an existing family-style recipe built around diezmillo de res (chuck). The
tomatillo-tomato salsa verde boiled then blended is the identity: a bright, slightly tart braise
that sets it apart from a brown beef stew. Potatoes go in late so they hold shape and soak up the
verde broth rather than disintegrating.
