steps:
  - Dice the onion, chop the broccoli into very small pieces, peel and slice the carrots.
  - Saute the onion in the butter in a large pot over medium until soft. Add the flour and cook ~2 min until it coats the bottom and turns light golden, this is the roux.
  - Add the chicken broth, whisking to dissolve the flour off the bottom. Bring to a simmer over medium-high, stirring often; it'll thicken to a gravy.
  - Turn to medium, whisk in the milk, smoked paprika, garlic powder, black pepper, and cayenne. Bring back to a simmer.
  - Add the broccoli and carrots, return to a simmer, and cook ~15 min, stirring often, until the carrots are tender.
  - Turn to medium-low and stir in the cheddar one handful at a time until melted. Taste and add salt if needed (depends on your broth).
