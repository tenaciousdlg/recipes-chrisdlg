steps:
  - Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add the chicken thighs and boil until no longer pink in the center and juices run clear, 15-20 min (165F internal). Drain, reserving 2 cups of the broth. Shred or chop the chicken and set aside.
  - Mix the salt, garlic powder, cumin, and black pepper together in a small bowl.
  - Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium-high. Add the calabaza, zucchini, yellow squash, tomato, and onion. Cook, stirring, until tender, ~10 min.
  - Add the corn, then the tomato sauce, the spice mix, and the chicken. Pour in the reserved 2 cups of chicken broth. Mix well.
  - Cover and simmer over medium heat 20-30 min.
