steps:
  - In a Dutch oven over medium-high, cook the diced bacon until nearly crispy. Set the bacon aside and reserve all but 1 tbsp of the fat.
  - Brown the cubed pork in the bacon fat. Set the pork aside.
  - Return the bacon and reserved fat to the pot. Saute the onion until translucent, then the garlic until fragrant.
  - Add the ground beef and brown thoroughly.
  - Combine 1 cup water, crushed tomatoes, sriracha, chili powder, cumin, oregano, and salt. Return the pork. Bring to a quick boil, cover, reduce to low, and simmer 1 hour.
  - Mix the masa harina with 1/4 cup water into a slurry; stir into the chili to thicken.
  - Add both beans and simmer 10 min. Finish with the lime juice off heat.

## Origin

A family chili recipe, distinct from the leaner turkey chili in the bank. The bacon-fat base,
cubed pork, masa thickening, and heavy sriracha make it a deep, rich, spicy cold-weather pot.
The lime finish ties it to the bright-heat palate even in a heavy dish.
