steps:
  - Salt and pepper both sides of the pork chops.
  - Combine the flour with the seasoned salt, black pepper, and cayenne. Dredge each chop both sides, set aside on a plate.
  - Heat the canola oil over medium to medium-high, add the butter. When hot, fry 3 chops at a time, 2-3 min the first side, flip and cook 1-2 min until golden and no pink juices remain.
  - Remove and repeat. Serve with smashed new potatoes.
