steps:
  - Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium-high. Saute onion, bell pepper, and garlic until softened, ~5 min.
  - Add ground turkey, break it up, brown ~6-7 min.
  - Stir in tomato paste, chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, and salt; toast 1 min to bloom the spices.
  - Add crushed tomatoes, both beans, broth, and the sweet potato if using. Bring to a simmer.
  - Simmer uncovered 25-35 min until thickened and the sweet potato is tender. Taste and adjust salt/heat. Longer simmering deepens it.
  - Serve topped with whatever you like, Greek yogurt (sour-cream swap), avocado, cheese, lime, cilantro.
