steps:
  - Whisk the marinade — greek yogurt, lemon zest and juice, minced garlic, olive oil, oregano, cumin, salt, pepper. The yogurt does the tenderizing and replaces most of the oil.
  - Toss thighs to coat fully. Marinate at least 30 min, ideally 2-4 hours in the fridge (yogurt marinades can go longer than acid-only ones without turning the meat mushy).
  - Bake at 425F ~20-25 min, or pan-sear 5-6 min/side, until 165F internal and the edges catch a little color. Rest 5 min before slicing.
  - Serve over rice or farro with cucumber, tomato, red onion — Greek-salad style — or alongside spinach. Tzatziki or the cucumber-yogurt sauce from the harissa bowls pairs cleanly.
