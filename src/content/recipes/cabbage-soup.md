steps:
  - Mince the garlic and dice the onion. Add both to a large soup pot along with the olive oil and saute over medium heat until the onions are soft and transparent.
  - While the onion and garlic are cooking, peel and slice the carrots, slice the celery, and dice the bell pepper. Add the carrots, celery, bell pepper, and frozen green beans to the pot, followed by the diced tomatoes (with their juices) and tomato sauce. Stir to combine.
  - Allow the vegetables to heat while you chop the cabbage into one-inch strips or squares, then add it to the pot. Add the vegetable broth, chopped parsley, paprika, oregano, thyme, and some freshly cracked pepper. Stir to combine.
  - Place a lid on the pot and bring it to a boil. Once boiling, reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until the cabbage is tender, about 20 minutes. Turn off the heat and add salt to taste, starting with about 1/2 tsp and adding more as needed.
  - Finish with the lemon juice to brighten it up right before serving.
