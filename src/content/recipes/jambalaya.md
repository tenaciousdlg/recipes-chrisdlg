steps:
  - Season the chicken with salt, pepper, and Cajun seasoning; season the shrimp with Cajun and keep refrigerated.
  - Heat 2 tsp oil in a large cast-iron or Dutch oven until smoking. Cook the chicken skin-side down first until golden both sides. Remove, discard skin.
  - Brown the andouille in the remaining fat over medium-high. Remove and set aside.
  - Add 3 tsp oil, then the onion, bell pepper, garlic, and salt; cook until the onion softens.
  - Add the rinsed rice, cook until translucent. Add the drained tomatoes, clam juice, and broth; bring to a simmer, uncovered.
  - Nestle the chicken back in, cook ~30 min until cooked through. Remove chicken, shred into bite pieces.
  - Stir the shrimp and sausage into the rice, cover, cook over low 3 min. Off heat, let stand until the shrimp are done and rice is tender. Stir in the shredded chicken and parsley; salt to taste.

The full layered jambalaya: sear chicken, brown andouille, build the rice in the rendered fat,
