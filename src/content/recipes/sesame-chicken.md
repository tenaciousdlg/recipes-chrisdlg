steps:
  - Whisk the egg, 2 tbsp cornstarch, and a pinch of salt and pepper in a large bowl. Cut the chicken thighs into 1-inch pieces and toss to coat in the egg mixture.
  - Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high until wavy. Add the chicken and all the egg mixture. Cook, stirring only occasionally, until golden brown and cooked through, ~7-10 min. Drain excess oil if desired.
  - "Make the sauce while the chicken cooks: stir together soy sauce, water, sesame oil, brown sugar, rice vinegar, grated ginger, minced garlic, sesame seeds, and the remaining 1 tbsp cornstarch."
  - Pour the sauce over the cooked chicken and toss. It thickens as soon as it hits the hot pan. Once coated and glossy, kill the heat.
  - Serve over jasmine rice, topped with sliced scallions.
