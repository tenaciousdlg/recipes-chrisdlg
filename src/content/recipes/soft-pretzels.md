steps:
  - Mix the 1.5 cups warm water with the yeast. Add the salt and sugar, keep mixing. Slowly work in the flour until the dough gets thick.
  - Knead with a little more flour a few minutes until smooth. Return to the bowl and let it sit in a 200F oven ~10 min to proof.
  - Shape into 6-8 pretzels.
  - Bring the 10 cups water and 1/2 cup baking soda to a boil. Lightly grease a baking sheet.
  - Dip each pretzel in the boiling baking-soda water 30 sec, using a large spoon or ladle. Let the excess drip off, place on the sheet.
  - Brush all over with the beaten egg (use it all), sprinkle with sea salt.
  - Bake at 425F for 10 min, then switch to broil for 5 min until deep golden.

## Origin

A homemade soft pretzel recipe from the family collection. The baking-soda-water boil before
baking is the key step that separates a real pretzel from bread. A fun weekend project; great
warm with mustard.
