steps:
  - "Combine the spice mix in a small bowl: garam masala, cumin, turmeric, smoked paprika, cayenne, salt, pepper."
  - Saute the onion, garlic, and ginger in the olive oil over medium until soft, ~3 min. Add the spice mix and cook 1 min more.
  - Add the frozen cauliflower and saute ~5 min until thawed and coated in spices.
  - Add the tomato sauce, chickpeas, and water. Stir, bring to a simmer, and cook until the cauliflower is tender.
  - Turn the heat low and stir in the heavy cream. Warm through, taste, and adjust salt. Serve over rice or with naan.
