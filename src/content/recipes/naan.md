steps:
  - Warm the milk slightly (not hot) and stir in the yeast and sugar. Let sit 5-10 min until foamy.
  - In a large bowl, whisk the flour, baking powder, and salt. Make a well, add the yogurt and the yeast mixture, and mix into a soft, slightly sticky dough. Knead ~5 min until smooth.
  - Cover and let rise in a warm spot until doubled, about 1 hour.
  - Divide into 6 balls. Roll each into a teardrop or oval shape, about 1/4-inch thick.
  - Heat a dry cast-iron or heavy skillet over medium-high. Cook each naan 1-2 min per side until puffed and charred in spots.
  - Brush with melted butter (mixed with the minced garlic, if using) right off the pan.
